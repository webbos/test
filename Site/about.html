    Development

Gorillas is an Open Source port of the original GORILLA.BAS to the iPhone platform.

It's a turn based single player and multi player arcade game where the objective is to blast your opponent away using carefully aimed bananas.

With a single touch of the display you determine the direction and speed of your banana's throw. Don't forget to take the wind into account!
